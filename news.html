How does digital cable work?


If your digital cable box is hooked up to a telephone line or high speed internet connection you can order Pay Per View Movies or Events using your remote control. If you do not have a telephone line or high speed internet connection connected to your digital cable box you must call Hotwire Customer Care at (561) 693-2082 Monday through Friday 7am-11pm or Saturday and Sunday 10am-6pm.
